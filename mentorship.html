Mentorship Program

Striving to impact the local Baltimore market and community, we intend to continue teaching and mentoring local Baltimore youth and partner with businesses and non-profits to teach relevant web development skills that they can apply for future work initiatives. We can help bridge the gap between under-privileged youth and technical education while impacting small businessâ€™ who might not have a giant budget to spend on their site. With our Mentorship program, we can help 2 local parties achieve their goals and create long-lasting value in our community.

Mentees will be hand-selected by the team twice per year and will have senior level resources to guide them on their way from foundational exercises to real life applications. A short-written essay and formal interview process will take place in the selection process. The mentorship program will last about 6 months with the potential to join the expanding team and/or assistance to find a new opportunity outside of this group.
